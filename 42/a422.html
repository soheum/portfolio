About
42 is a pregnancy toolkit that is used by not only pregnant women, but also their husbands. This toolkit enhances communi-
cation and understanding between the wife and the husband by having cards that contain information about pregnancy. Also by coloring each card, it enhances pregnant women’s prenatal education as it completes the whole photo in the end of the usage.  
Challenge
42 was a project that required a lot of user research. Since none of us in our team had experience with pregnancy, we had to utilize different user research methods in order to understand the main user of our service. Furthermore, we had to think of a completely new solution either than traditional pregnancy books or applications that are already existant in the market, which made us develop a toolkit.

42 was a project with the most unfamiliar user group that I have experienced. Since it is difficult for people in their early twenties to experience the concept of pregnancy, we approached this project with no prejudices at all. This allowed us to interpret interview results purely focusing on the research content. With this user research results, we had gone through extensive brainstorming sessions in order to develop a solution that will differentiate from mere pregnancy related books or online contents.

Also, unlike outcomes such as a mobile application or an online website, a toolkit was a whole new experience for our team to tackle. Since a toolkit does not involve any online communication methods, we redefined what a user flow will look like when dealing with offline contents.
