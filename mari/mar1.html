About
mARimekko Shop Anywhere is a service that makes Marimekko customer’s experience easier and more dynamic with providing combinations of Marimekko products and its patterns through a mobile AR application.
Challenge
Marimekko currently does not have a mobile application, which lacks in connectivity with people worldwide. Also, due to its relatively high price compared to other brands, younger generations often face difficulties in approaching the brand. Our solution tackles how Marimekko can attract new customers with AR along with keeping its original customers.

Working in such an international team in Helsinki allowed me to broaden my point of view. Starting from the most basic communication methods, it was a completely new atmosphere for me to work on. Unlike projects that I did in Korea, where I can slightly grasp or expect how the project will develop, this Marimekko Designathon was totally unexpected. And I was able to experience how to cooperate and collaborate different ideas in this unexpected environment.

Furthermore, presenting in front of Marimekko Team at Slush was an opportunity to express my ideas and receive direct feedback from actual stakeholders. I was able to think from a company’s point of view on how my service will work or influence their customers.
