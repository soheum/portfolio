About
This data visualization project analyzes football manager’s leadership and their characteristics in planning a match’s strategy in treble teams. Comparing the results with double football teams, it analyzes the slightest difference in making a football team a double or a treble winner.  
Challenge
Currently, there are various results that analyze football player’s statistics with their match data. However, there aren’t many results with manager’s statistics. Our team had to set new standards that will measure a football manger’s role and their leadership in a team with data.
