About
This calendar series established a system through Buckminster Fuller’s 3d geodesic dome. Each month of the calendar analyzes how this 3d hemisphere structure developed by an American architect Fuller, developed from a 2D cubotahedron shape to a 3d dome.
Challenge
Fuller’s geodesic dome goes through various stages: a cuboctahedron, Jitterburg Transformation, geodesic dome, tensegrity structure. This series illustrates how each stage contributes to the making of a geodesic dome, and how each of these parts affect in creating a visual system.

This project challenged me to visually interpret the transformation of 2 dimensional shapes to a 3 dimensional dome. I had to construct a new visual system that will portray this process of transformation and give different variations depending on the month. Along with studying how Buckminster Fuller developed his algorithm, I added a special personal touch in my calendar series. Also, not only dealing with print materials, I designed and made the package as well, which was a new medium for me to express my visual system.
